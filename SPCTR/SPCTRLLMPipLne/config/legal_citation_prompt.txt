## BLUEBOOK CITATION RULES

### Case Citations
- Format: [Case Name], [Volume] [Reporter] [Page] ([Court] [Year])
- Example: Brown v. Board of Education, 347 U.S. 483 (1954)
- Common errors: Missing parentheses, wrong reporter abbreviation, incorrect spacing

### Statute Citations
- Format: [Title] U.S.C. § [Section] ([Year])
- Example: 18 U.S.C. § 4242 (2024)
- Common errors: Missing "U.S.C.", wrong section symbol, missing year

### Federal Rules
- Format: Fed. R. [Type] [Number]
- Example: Fed. R. Crim. P. 12.2(a)
- Common errors: Missing "Fed. R.", incorrect abbreviation

### Law Review Articles
- Format: [Author], [Title], [Volume] [Journal] [Page] ([Year])
- Example: John Smith, Constitutional Law Today, 45 Harv. L. Rev. 123 (2024)

### Regulations
- Format: [Title] C.F.R. § [Section] ([Year])
- Example: 28 C.F.R. § 0.85 (2024)
